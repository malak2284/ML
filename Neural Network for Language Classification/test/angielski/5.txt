Family is at the center of Italian life. Family bonds are strong and enduring, and family gatherings are often occasions for joy and celebration. The kitchen is the heart of the home, where cherished recipes are shared from generation to generation, creating precious moments of togetherness.

Football is a national passion in Italy. Matches are followed fervently, and cheering is an exciting experience that brings people of all ages together. Local derbies and international showdowns are unmissable events that animate the streets and bars across the country.
